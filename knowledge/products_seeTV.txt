What is
CTV?
Connected TV (CTV) refers to any TV connected to the internet, whether a Smart TV or one paired with devices like set-top boxes, TV sticks, or gaming consoles. With our Smart Exposure Engine (SeeTV), we ensure your brand gets strategic, high-impact exposure right on these connected screens where viewer attention is at its peak.
Smart TVs
Internet-connected TVs that support streaming apps and digital content without external devices.
OTT
Video content streamed over the internet through apps, without relying on traditional cable or satellite TV.
Streaming Media Devices
Connected set-top-boxes and dongles e.g. Amazon Fire TV, Apple TV, Roku, Chromecast.
Game Consoles
Gaming devices with internet access that support streaming apps and media playback.
IP-Enabled STBs
Play-TV STBs that provide access to 3rd party OTT apps and video players.
Managed services STBs
Play-TV STBs that only provide access to operator-controlled services.
